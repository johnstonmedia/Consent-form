Privacy Policy

Last updated:

We keep this simple: we only collect what we need to run this consent form, we never share your personal information publicly, and all data is deleted when your consent expires.

Who we are

Johnston Media is a photography and videography business based in New South Wales, Australia. This consent form is operated by Johnston Media for the purpose of obtaining and managing social media consent from participants and their parents or guardians.

For any privacy enquiries, contact us at wjohnston.media@gmail.com.

What information we collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate this consent form:

  • Your name and email address to create and identify your account
  • For parent/guardian forms: the student's name, school, sport or team, year level, and date of birth
  • For personal consent forms: your name, email, phone number, date of birth, school or organisation, and sport or team
  • Your consent selections (which permissions you have granted)
  • Your digital signature and the date it was provided
  • Any special instructions or restrictions you choose to include

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Maintain your account so you can log in, view, edit, or withdraw your consent at any time
  • Record a valid consent that Johnston Media can refer to before publishing any media
  • Allow Johnston Media to understand the permissions you have and have not granted

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to managing your consent.

How long we keep your data

Your personal information is stored only for the duration of your active consent period — six months from the date you signed the form. Once your consent expires or you withdraw it, your data is no longer required and will be removed.

Your account credentials (email and password) are stored in your browser's local storage on your own device. They are not held on external servers unless this site is connected to a backend database in a future update.

Who can see your information

Your personal information is never shared publicly. Access is strictly limited to:

  • You — via your personal account dashboard
  • The Johnston Media administrator — solely for the purpose of verifying consent before publishing media

We do not sell, rent, trade, or disclose your personal information to any third parties, advertisers, or external organisations.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you at any time via your account dashboard
  • Correct any inaccurate information by editing your consent form
  • Withdraw your consent at any time, which stops Johnston Media from using new media of you or your child going forward
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us directly

To exercise any of these rights, log in to your account or contact us at wjohnston.media@gmail.com.

Cookies & tracking

This site does not use cookies or any third-party tracking tools. Account data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) on your own device and is not transmitted to any external server.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of this consent form after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact Johnston Media at wjohnston.media@gmail.com
