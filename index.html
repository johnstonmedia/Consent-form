Privacy Policy

Last updated:

We keep this simple: we only collect what we need to run this consent form, we never share your personal information publicly, and all data is removed when your consent expires.

Who we are

Johnston Media is a photography and videography business based in New South Wales, Australia. This consent form is operated by Johnston Media for the purpose of obtaining and managing social media consent from participants and their parents or guardians. Contact: wjohnston.media@gmail.com

What information we collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate this consent form:

  • Your name and email address to create and identify your account
  • For parent/guardian forms: the student's name, school, sport or team, year level, and date of birth
  • For personal consent forms: your name, email, phone number, date of birth, school or organisation, and sport or team
  • Your consent selections, digital signature, and the date it was provided
  • Any special instructions or restrictions you choose to include

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to maintain your account, record your consent, and allow Johnston Media to verify permissions before publishing any media. We do not use your information for marketing or any unrelated purpose.

How long we keep your data

Your personal information is stored only for the duration of your active consent period — six months from the date you signed the form. Once your consent expires or you withdraw it, your data is no longer required and will be removed. Account authentication is managed securely by Google Firebase.

Who can see your information

Your personal information is never shared publicly. Access is strictly limited to you (via your account dashboard) and the Johnston Media administrator solely for verifying consent. We do not sell, rent, or disclose your information to any third parties.

Your rights

  • Access your information at any time via your account dashboard
  • Correct inaccurate information by editing your consent form
  • Withdraw consent at any time from your dashboard
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us directly

Cookies & tracking

This site does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. Authentication is handled by Google Firebase which uses secure session tokens to keep you signed in.

Questions? Contact Johnston Media at wjohnston.media@gmail.com
